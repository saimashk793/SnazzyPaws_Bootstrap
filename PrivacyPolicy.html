Privacy Policy

At Snazzy Paws Grooming Studio, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

Information Collection

We collect personal information that you provide to us voluntarily when you:

  • Book an appointment
  • Contact us via email or phone
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

Use of Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you promotional materials and updates

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our website uses SSL encryption to ensure the safe transmission of your data.

Third-Party Services

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our operations, such as email marketing and payment processing. These third parties are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@snazzypaws.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@snazzypaws.com.
